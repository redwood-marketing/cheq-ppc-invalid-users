Learn how ActiveBatch is driving digital transformation. faqs: What is business process automation?: Business process automation (BPA) is the use of software to automate manual tasks, create streamlined workflows, improve efficiency and eliminate manual errors. BPA can be used to accomplish a variety of tasks, including order-to-cash accounting, supply chain management, insurance claims processing, employee onboarding and much more. Learn more about business process automation. What is the difference between RPA and BPA?: Business process automation (BPA) and robotic process automation (RPA) are both technologies that use software to automate tasks and improve business efficiency. BPA optimizes business processes and workflows, enabling the orchestration and monitoring of complex tasks that span multiple systems, applications and endpoints. RPA is used to create bots that automate manual, repetitive tasks. RPA can handle tasks such as data entry, transaction processing, report generation, copying and pasting information and even reading and verifying invoice data.
